2.137 Pragma Psect_Object

Syntax: 

    pragma Psect_Object (
         [Internal =>] LOCAL_NAME,
      [, [External =>] EXTERNAL_SYMBOL]
      [, [Size     =>] EXTERNAL_SYMBOL]);
    
    EXTERNAL_SYMBOL ::=
      IDENTIFIER
    | static_string_EXPRESSION

This pragma is identical in effect to pragma Common_Object.
